ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Aoi. I'm a second-year Master of Information Management Systems student at UC Berkeley, focusing on data science and product management. I attended the University of Tokyo, where I earned a Master’s degree in Public Policy and a Bachelor’s degree in Law. I have spent years working for the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare, analyzing data and revising laws and regulations. In my free time, I have been teaching Japanese to other students on campus and enjoying hikes around Berkeley.
